How to Find Your IDs

A step-by-step guide to locate your FPL Manager ID and Mini League ID

1

Finding Your Manager ID

Your Manager ID is a unique number assigned to your FPL account.

Steps:
  1. Go to fantasy.premierleague.com
  2. Log in to your FPL account
  3. Click on your Profile or Team tab
  4. Look at the URL in your browser's address bar
fantasy.premierleague.com/entry/1234567/team/

The number between /entry/ and /team/ is your Manager ID ✅

In this example: 1234567 is the Manager ID

2

Finding Your Mini League ID

Your Mini League ID can be found in your private or public league page.

Steps:
  1. Go to fantasy.premierleague.com
  2. Log in to your FPL account
  3. Click on Leagues in the sidebar or main menu
  4. Select the Mini League you want to spy on
  5. Look at the URL in your browser's address bar
fantasy.premierleague.com/leagues/classic/12345/standings/

The number between /classic/ and /standings/ is your League ID ✅

In this example: 12345 is the League ID

Summary

Manager ID

Found in your team profile URL

fantasy.premierleague.com/entry/YOUR_ID/team/
League ID

Found in your league standings URL

fantasy.premierleague.com/leagues/classic/YOUR_ID/standings/
Pro Tip: You can have multiple mini leagues! Each league has its own unique ID. Make sure to get the ID of the league you want to spy on.
