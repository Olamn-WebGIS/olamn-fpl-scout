Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 9, 2026

1. Introduction

FPL Scout Company operates the FPL Scout website. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and otherwise handle your information in relation to our website and services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information in various ways, including:

  • Automatically Collected Information: When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information about your device and browsing activity, including IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on pages.
  • Information from Google Analytics: We use Google Analytics to track user interactions and website performance.
  • Information from Google AdSense: We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may collect information about your browsing activity.
  • Manager ID Data: If you choose to sync your FPL Manager ID, we store this information to provide personalized predictions.
  • Cookies: We use cookies to enhance your experience and analyze website performance.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve our services and website functionality
  • To display personalized content and recommendations
  • To analyze website usage and trends through Google Analytics
  • To serve targeted advertisements through Google AdSense
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Third-Party Services

Our website uses third-party services that may collect information about you:

  • Google AdSense: Displays targeted advertisements. Google has its own Privacy Policy governing the data it collects.
  • Google Analytics: Tracks website usage and provides analytics. Google does not personally identify you.
  • Bootstrap CDN: We use Bootstrap for website styling and functionality.

5. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to store information about your preferences and history. You can control cookies through your browser settings. Third-party services may also use cookies for their purposes.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of certain data collection and marketing communications

8. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. You use our website at your own risk.

9. Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child, we will delete it promptly.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the updated policy on our website and updating the "Last Updated" date.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at:

Email: info@fplscout.name.ng
Website: www.fplscout.name.ng

12. GDPR and CCPA Compliance

If you are located in the European Union or California, you have additional rights under GDPR and CCPA respectively. We are committed to complying with these regulations. Please contact us for more information about your rights.
